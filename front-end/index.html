MillionVault is a community powered decentralized staking platform for TechLead's Million (MM) token. MillionVault was founded by web3.builders (as millionaires). The smart contract for MillionVault is located at 0x53684d5bcB3eEB589ff88eD6054B3301F55FbCde

Staking and Unstaking converts your $MM token to and from $MV at a ratio of 1:1. There is a 10% fee applied to both Stake and Unstake input amounts. You earn dividends based on trading volume and your overall weight in the pool. Increase your earnings by inviting new stakers and reinvesting earned dividends.
